How to play:
Click "w" button or press "w" to play Bass
Click "a" button or press "a" to play Kick
Click "s" button or press "s" to play Clap
Click "d" button or press "d" to play Hihat
Click "j" button or press "j" to play Snare
Click "k" button or press "k" to play Open Hat
Click "l" button or press "l" to play Perc

Hip Hop Drum Kit

Made in Soweto.
